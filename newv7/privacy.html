Privacy Policy

How Champion Auto Finance collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Who we are

Champion Auto Finance (“Champion,” “we,” “us”) is a New Jersey–based financing partner licensed with the New Jersey Department of Banking and Insurance. We coordinate dealer financing and lease buyouts between customers, dealer partners, and qualified lenders. Champion Auto Finance is not a lender. This policy explains what information we collect through championautofinance.com and in the course of arranging financing, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

Information you provide. When you submit a form, call, or email us, we collect what you share — typically your name, email address, phone number, and details about your vehicle, lease, or financing goal.

Application information. If you move forward with a financing request, we collect the information needed to structure and process the deal — such as vehicle and payoff details, employment and income information, and credit-related information — either directly from you or through the dealer handling your deal.

Automatic information. Like most websites, our hosting provider records basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, and may set performance cookies. We do not run advertising trackers.

How we use your information

  • To review your request and communicate with you about it
  • To structure your deal and match it with qualified lenders
  • To coordinate with the dealer involved in your purchase or buyout
  • To meet licensing, record-keeping, and other legal obligations
  • To operate and improve our website and services

Who we share it with

We share personal information only as needed to arrange your financing: with qualified lenders and financial institutions that underwrite your request, with the dealer partner involved in your deal, and with service providers that host our website and systems. We may also disclose information when required by law or regulation. We do not sell your personal information.

How long we keep it

We retain deal records for as long as needed to service your request and to satisfy the record-keeping requirements that apply to licensed financing businesses in New Jersey, and then dispose of them securely.

How we protect it

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal information. No website or transmission method is completely secure, so please share sensitive details through our forms or by phone rather than regular email where possible.

Your choices and rights

You may contact us at any time to review, correct, or request deletion of the personal information we hold about you, subject to records we are required to keep. You can also ask us to stop sending marketing communications.

Children

Our services are for adults. The site is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be posted on this page with its effective date.
