FAQs

Questions, answered

Straight answers about who we are, what we do, and how a Champion deal comes together — for dealers and buyers alike.

Is Champion Auto Finance a lender?

No. Champion Auto Finance is a licensed financing partner. Approvals, terms, and rates are subject to lender underwriting, credit approval, and applicable law.

What types of financing do you handle?

We coordinate dealer financing, lease buyouts, and auto refinancing, connecting applicants with qualified lenders across multiple credit tiers.

Do you work with dealers and consumers?

Yes. Dealers can submit deals through the dealer workflow, and consumers may apply directly for buyouts or refinancing.

Are you licensed?

Champion Auto Finance operates under NJ DOBI regulation and structures deals with attention to applicable lending and privacy requirements.

What credit tiers do you work with?

We work with banks and lenders across multiple credit tiers. Eligibility is determined by the lender based on applicant profile and deal structure.

How long does funding take?

Timing depends on the lender and how quickly documentation and stipulations are completed. A representative will provide a deal-specific estimate.

Still have a question?

Ask us directly — real people, straight answers. Tell us what you’re trying to do and we’ll point you the right way.
