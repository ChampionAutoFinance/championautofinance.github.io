FAQs

Questions, answered

Straight answers about who we are, what we do, and how a Champion deal comes together — for dealers and buyers alike.

Is Champion Auto Finance a lender?

No. Champion Auto Finance is a licensed financing partner. We coordinate financing with qualified lenders — approvals, terms, and rates are subject to lender underwriting, credit approval, and applicable laws.

What types of financing do you handle?

We coordinate dealer financing and lease buyouts — connecting applicants with qualified lenders across multiple credit tiers.

Are you licensed and compliant?

Yes — we operate under NJ DOBI regulation and structure every deal to applicable state lending and privacy law.

Do you work with both dealers and consumers?

Yes. Dealers structure and submit deals through our workspace, and consumers can apply directly for a lease buyout. Both get the same transparent, professional standard.

What exactly is a lease buyout?

A lease buyout is financing the residual (payoff) amount of your lease so you own the vehicle instead of returning it. We coordinate a loan for that amount with a qualified lender.

Can a lease buyout really save me money?

Often, yes — you finance the vehicle’s residual (payoff) amount instead of paying excess-mileage and wear-and-tear return charges, and you keep a car whose history you already know. Final terms are always subject to lender approval.

Does reaching out affect my credit?

Asking for guidance and a review costs nothing and carries no obligation. A formal lender application may involve a credit inquiry — we’ll always tell you before that step.

What credit do I need to qualify?

We work with lenders across multiple credit tiers, so there’s no single cutoff. The best way to know is to send a few details and let us review your situation.

What should I have ready to apply?

A valid ID, proof of income, your vehicle details (year, make, model, mileage, VIN), and your lease payoff or current loan statement help us move quickly.

Do I need my own bank relationships?

No. That’s the point — you reach multiple qualified lenders through one submission, without maintaining a shelf of direct bank relationships yourself.

How do I submit a deal?

You structure the customer, vehicle, and terms in a dealer-first workspace — one organized worksheet — and we take it from there through matching, stipulations, and funding.

How fast do deals fund?

Approvals fund fast — quick and easy. Timing depends on the lender and the completeness of the file, which is exactly why we help handle stipulations up front.

Still have a question?

Ask us directly — real people, straight answers. Tell us what you’re trying to do and we’ll point you the right way.
