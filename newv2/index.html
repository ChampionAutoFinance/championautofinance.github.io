Auto Financing, Handled Correctly

A licensed financing partner coordinating dealer financing, lease buyouts, and auto refinancing — with a clear, documented process from application to funding.

Notice Champion Auto Finance is not a lender. Financing approvals, terms, and rates are subject to lender underwriting, credit approval, and applicable law.

Services

Dealer Financing
Dealers submit financed deals through one point of access to a network of qualified lenders across multiple credit tiers.
Lease Buyouts
Purchase a leased vehicle before the lease term ends without unexpected end-of-term charges.
Auto Refinancing
Refinance an existing auto loan to pursue a lower rate or a lower monthly payment.

How It Works

  1. Submit the deal

    Dealers structure a deal or buyers apply. Customer, vehicle, and terms are recorded on one worksheet.

  2. Review and match

    Credit, income, and deal structure are reviewed and matched with qualified lenders.

  3. Structure and sign

    Terms are documented in plain language and processed in compliance with applicable law.

  4. Funded

    Approved deals are funded. The dealer is paid and the buyer takes delivery.
