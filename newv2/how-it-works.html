How It Works

A clear, documented process from application to funding — organized and straightforward for both dealers and buyers.

The Process

  1. Submit the deal

    Dealers structure and submit a deal, or buyers apply directly. Customer information, vehicle details, and proposed terms are recorded on a single worksheet.

  2. Review and match

    Credit, income, and deal structure are reviewed and matched with our network of qualified lenders across multiple credit tiers.

  3. Structure and sign

    Terms are presented in plain language and documented. Stipulations and compliance requirements are handled, and each step is explained.

  4. Funded

    Approved deals are funded by the lender. The dealer is paid and the buyer takes delivery.
