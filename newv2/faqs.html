Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Champion Auto Finance and how the process works.

Is Champion Auto Finance a lender?
No. Champion Auto Finance is a licensed financing partner. Approvals, terms, and rates are subject to lender underwriting, credit approval, and applicable law.
What types of financing do you handle?
We coordinate dealer financing, lease buyouts, and auto refinancing, connecting applicants with qualified lenders across multiple credit tiers.
Do you work with both dealers and consumers?
Yes. Dealers structure and submit deals through our workspace, and consumers may apply directly. Both receive the same transparent, professional process.
Are you licensed and compliant?
Yes. We operate under NJ DOBI regulation and structure every deal in compliance with applicable state lending and privacy law.
What credit tiers do you work with?
We work with banks and lenders across multiple credit tiers. Eligibility is determined by the lender based on the applicant and the deal structure.
How long does funding take?
Timing depends on the lender and how quickly documentation and stipulations are completed. A representative will provide an estimate for your specific deal.
What area do you serve?
Champion Auto Finance is based in New Jersey and licensed under NJ DOBI.
How do I get started?
Submit an inquiry on the Contact page or call (732) 618-2036, and a representative will follow up.
