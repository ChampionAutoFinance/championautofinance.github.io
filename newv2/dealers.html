Dealer Financing

Submit financed deals through one point of access to a network of qualified lenders. No need to maintain direct bank relationships.

Program Benefits

  • Structure and submit deals in a single dealer workspace.
  • One point of access to multiple lenders across credit tiers.
  • Compliance and documentation support on every deal.
  • Faster approvals and organized funding.

How Dealers Work With Us

1. Enroll
Complete a brief dealer enrollment so we can set up your workspace and confirm program details.
2. Submit deals
Enter the customer, vehicle, and proposed terms. Attach required documentation.
3. Get matched
We review the deal and match it with qualified lenders, then handle stipulations.
4. Get funded
On approval, the lender funds the deal and the dealership is paid.
