Lease Buyouts & Auto Refinancing

Keep the vehicle you have and pursue better terms. Champion Auto Finance coordinates lease buyouts and refinancing with qualified lenders.

Lease Buyouts

A lease buyout is the purchase of a leased vehicle, financed as an auto loan, before or at the end of the lease term.

  • Purchase the vehicle before the lease ends without unexpected end-of-term charges.
  • Avoid mileage and wear-and-tear penalties that may apply on return.
  • Financing coordinated across multiple credit tiers.

Auto Refinancing

Refinancing replaces an existing auto loan with a new loan, which may offer a lower interest rate or a lower monthly payment.

  • Pursue a lower rate or a reduced monthly payment.
  • Adjust the loan term to fit your budget.
  • Licensed, documented guidance from application to funding.

Buyout vs. Refinancing

 Lease BuyoutAuto Refinancing
Current statusYou lease the vehicleYou have a loan on the vehicle
GoalOwn the vehicle you leaseImprove the terms of your loan
ResultNew auto loan to purchase the carNew auto loan replacing the old one
Notice Champion Auto Finance is not a lender. Eligibility, rates, and terms are determined by the lender and are subject to credit approval and applicable law.
