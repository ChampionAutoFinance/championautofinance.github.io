How It Works

From application to funded

One clear path, whether you’re a dealer submitting a deal or a buyer applying for a lease buyout or refinance. Organized, documented, and easy to understand at every step.

Start Now → Walk the steps
1

Submit the deal

Dealers structure a deal in a dealer-first workspace; buyers apply online for a buyout or refinance. Customer, vehicle, and terms come together in one clean worksheet — no scattered paperwork.

2

Review & match

We review credit, income, and deal structure, then match the file with our network of qualified lenders across multiple credit tiers — so it lands where it has the best chance to fund.

3

Structure & sign

Terms come back clear and documented — rate, payment, and stipulations explained in plain language. Everything is structured to be fully compliant before a signature is ever requested.

4

Funded

Clean approvals fund fast. The dealer gets paid, the buyer drives, and the file holds up — verified, documented, and built to last.
