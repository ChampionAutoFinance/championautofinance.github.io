A clear path from application to funding.

Every deal is organized around the same fundamentals: complete information, lender-fit review, documented terms, and clean funding.

01

Submit the deal

Dealers structure and submit a deal, or buyers apply directly. Customer information, vehicle details, and proposed terms are recorded in one file.

02

Review and match

Credit, income, and deal structure are reviewed and matched with qualified lenders across multiple credit tiers.

03

Structure and sign

Terms are presented in plain language. Stipulations and compliance requirements are handled before signing.

What you will need.

The exact documentation depends on the lender and program, but most files begin with the same core pieces.

  • Valid government-issued photo identification.
  • Proof of income, such as pay stubs, bank statements, or equivalent documentation.
  • Vehicle year, make, model, VIN, mileage, and sale price or payoff amount.
  • Current loan or lease account details for buyouts and refinancing.
Please note: Required documentation may vary by lender and program. A representative will confirm exactly what is needed for your deal.
