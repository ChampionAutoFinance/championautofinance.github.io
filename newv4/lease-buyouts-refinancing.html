Keep the vehicle you have. Rework the financing around it.

Champion Auto Finance coordinates lease buyouts and auto refinancing with qualified lenders across multiple credit tiers.

Lease Buyouts

A lease buyout lets you purchase the vehicle you already lease, financed as an auto loan before or at the end of the lease term.

  • Keep the vehicle you know.
  • Avoid potential mileage and wear charges on return.
  • Coordinate payoff, lender review, and signing.

Auto Refinancing

Refinancing replaces an existing auto loan with a new loan, which may offer a lower rate, lower payment, or revised term when eligible.

  • Review the current loan and payoff.
  • Compare eligible lender options.
  • Complete documentation through funding.

Buyout vs. refinance.

The right path depends on whether the vehicle is currently leased or already financed.

QuestionLease BuyoutAuto Refinancing
Current statusYou lease the vehicle.You have an auto loan.
Primary goalPurchase the vehicle you lease.Improve or revise current loan terms.
ResultA new auto loan used to buy the leased vehicle.A new auto loan replacing the existing loan.
